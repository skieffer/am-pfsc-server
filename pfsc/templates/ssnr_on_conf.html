Server-Side Note Recording

WARNING: This will erase any notes you have currently saved in the browser!

As an alternative to recording your study notes and checkmarks in the browser, you may opt in to having them recorded by the server. This is intended to overcome the inherent limitations of recording in the browser, such as the need to manage local storage, and dependence on which computer or browser you are using.
